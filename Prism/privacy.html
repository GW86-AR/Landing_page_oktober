PRISM Workspace Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Data Collection

PRISM Workspace operates as a local-first application. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, shortcuts, or notes to any external servers. All information created within the app is stored locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

The application interacts with the Microsoft Store for license validation and in-app purchases. This process is governed by Microsoft's privacy policy. We do not have access to your payment information or Microsoft account credentials.

3. Local Storage

Your workspace data (contexts, folders, links) is stored in your computer's local storage. Deleting the application data or the application itself will result in the loss of this information unless you have a manual backup.

4. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: support@oktoberlab.com
